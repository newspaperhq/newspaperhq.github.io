Privacy Policy


What information do we collect?

Newspaper only collects the team authentication token (required to authenticate Newspaper with your Slack), and the information required to identify what channels to post topic content to. Otherwise no other data is kept.

Any questions or feedback contact us at hello@newspaper.io
